Famous signers

Thomas Jefferson
Thomas jefferson, more formally known as Tommy J amongst his friends, signed the decleration of independence on a dre from John Hancock (more on him later). A delegate from virginia, he would go on to serve as the third president of the united states.
John Adams
John Adams, representing Massachusetts, was a vocal advocate for indipendence in the contitental congress. He became the second president of the united states and played a key role in both the writing of and the adoption of the foundational documents of America.
